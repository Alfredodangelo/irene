1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites save on a user's device during browsing. They may be "session" cookies (deleted when the browser is closed) or "persistent" cookies (stored for a defined period).

Disabling technical cookies may impair the correct functioning of certain site features (e.g. booking forms, payments).
