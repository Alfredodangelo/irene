Aftercare

First Hours

  • Remove protective film after a few hours
  • Wash gently with neutral soap and warm water
  • Pat dry (do not rub)
  • Do not re-cover with film

Days 2-14

  • Wash morning and evening
  • Apply thin cream morning and evening
  • DO NOT scratch (even if it itches!)
  • Avoid direct sun, swimming pools, sea

From Day 14

  • Continue washing and cream morning and evening
  • Surface healing is complete
  • Always use SPF 50+ sunscreen on the tattoo

For any doubts during healing, contact me on WhatsApp!
